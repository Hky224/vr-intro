Examples of VR Devices in the Market

Facebook Oculus Quest 2

Oculus Quest 2 is a VR headset released on 13th October 2020, published by Facebook Reality Labs. It is a headset that has up to 256 GB of storage, running on android operating sy stem with an LCD monitor which has a resolution of 1832 x 1920 pixels that can run up to 120hz refresh rate for better user experience. It also has a pair of touch controllers which are motion controllers for users to interact with the system’s interface.

HTC VIVE Pro 2

VIVE Pro 2 brings immersive virtual reality experience for the users. It is released on 3rd June 2021, Developed by Dassault Systèmes. The monitor of the headset has 120hz, with 120° field of view. The resolution of the headset is 2448 x 2448 pixels per eye, which is best for gaming. The motion tracking of the device is also designed to be sensitive in order to bring the best experience for users.

More information and purchasing details about VIVE Pro 2

Sony PlayStation VR

PlayStation VR is designed for gaming usage on PlayStation 4 (PS4) console. It is released on 13th October 2016, by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is required to connect to a PS4 to work. The headset has a OLED monitor with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. It also has up 120hz refresh rate, can be adjusted downward. It also has about 100° field of view in order to bring users a immersive experience of gaming.
