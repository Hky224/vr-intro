Virtual Reality (VR)

A computer-generated 3D environment on the Internet...



With the rapid development of Metaverse and internet technology, simulating scenes, sounds and objects are more similar and mature compared with our real world. A VR headset has a head-mounted display that includes a small screen in front of our eyes, it allows users to have a feeling of being immersed in the virtual environment similar to reality. By using the Virtual Reality headset or helmet, we can perceive the 3D environment and chat with others online using Internet Relay Chat (IRC) and Hyper Text Transfer Protocol (HTTP). It has a wide application in the field of multimedia entertainment, video games, education and business on the Internet that can allow users to connect with each other.



When user looks around on the virtual internet environment, they can move around and interact with some virtual features or items. For example, if the user uses virtual reality to immerse themselves in a video game, they become one of the characters, and they can discover the map of the video game by walking around in the map and interacting with other characters or users inside with MMOG games.

Although it looks futuristic, the first virtual reality device was invented in the 1950s which called Sensorama, that is a machine have a built-in seat allow a user to sit and watch 3D movies, and creates vibration and odor to provide a feeling of the reality.
