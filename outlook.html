VR in Education and Training



A more experiential way of learning can be brought out by educating with VR technology. For example, kindergarten student can discover and learn new objects in an interactive way instead of seeing a 2-D picture. Medical students could have a more realistic learning experience within virtual perspective on human bodies.



For training, simulating the driving process in heavy urban traffic, preparing people for real roads, providing base knowledge and experiencing procedures of safety driving is the most secured method. With the help of adequate trainings with driving simulation software, less accidents would be prevented.


VR in Entertainment



Gaming industry in VR has already well-developed nowadays. However, there are still a large space for improvement. Therefore, the future of VR gaming is worth to be awaited. By achieving things that human being is not able to do in reality, users will be experienced a high level of entertainment in the future. Also, more gears are expected to develop in order to assist this technology becoming more realistic, such as racing wheel tools that we can buy now.


VR in Real Estate


If you are going to rent or purchase an apartment, it must be time-consuming to visit different houses until finding your dear house. But by putting on a virtual reality headset, you can both observe the existing location and see how your fully-structured property will looks like in the future. The property could be located in another country, but you are still able to get a tour without leaving your own apartment.
